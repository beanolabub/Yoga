Yoga For Everyone!
with Yogi Sandy Willmott based in Lincoln, Lincolnshire.

I began practicing yoga about 25 years ago to try something different from 'feeling the burn' of the aerobics classes of the day. Little did I know that this would send me on a never-ending journey of physical, mental and spiritual challenges.

I soon discovered that yoga wasn't just another gym class and, realising that practising yoga regularly was beneficial on every level, I wanted to share this with others.

So, I trained as a yoga teacher, and now hold a British Wheel of Yoga BWYQ Level 4 diploma in teaching yoga. I have a range of clients : from people who have never set foot anywhere near a yoga mat, to those who have their own regular practice. I am fully insured and teach private students as well as my own group classes, open to all. I also teach chair yoga classes on Zoom.

I am dedicated to furthering my understanding and knowledge of this amazing ancient practice, which has enhanced my life. I participate in regular workshops with some of the world's best teachers, including Jason Crandell, Howard Napper, Tara Styles and Peter Blackaby.

FAQ

What do I to wear for a yoga class? Comfortable clothing you can move around in. A sweatshirt or fleece and socks for warmth during relaxation. Ideally, we practice barefoot.

What should I bring to a yoga class? A yoga mat, a yoga strap, two yoga bricks, a small blanket and water.

What to expect at a yoga class:

  • Basic breathing: helps calm the mind
    helps you focus and prepare for your class
  • Warm-up session: to warm and mobilise joints and muscles
  • Postures: dynamic (moving in and out with the breath), or static (held for up to 30 seconds)
  • Counter poses: work the opposite areas of the previous poses, and provide a balance to the overall practice
  • Relaxation: a guided practice that allows the body and mind to relax

Yoga can help you to:

  • Improve flexibility
  • build muscle strength
  • perfect your posture
  • protect your spine
  • boost immunity
  • focus your mind
  • improve balance
  • sleep better
  • breathe more efficiently
  • relax and ease tension
